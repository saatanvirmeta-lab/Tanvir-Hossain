eCommerce • Meta Ads

Automotive Parts Scaling Case Study

Scaled a brand-new automotive eCommerce business from zero to 964 purchases using a structured Facebook Ads funnel. Objective was to build a scalable and cost-efficient system from scratch.

964Purchases
$1.75M+Revenue

Industry: Automotive Parts (eCommerce)
Platform: Meta Ads (Facebook)

Results at a Glance

  • 964 Website Purchases generated
  • Conversion value: $1,755,167
  • 626,451 users reached
  • $22 – $33 average cost per purchase
  • $29,318 total ad spend
  • Scaled from a brand-new business with no prior data

Client Overview

The client launched a new automotive parts eCommerce business with no existing sales history. The goal was to build a reliable and scalable sales engine using Facebook Ads, while maintaining cost efficiency and stable performance from day one.

The Challenge

Launching paid ads for a new brand presented several critical challenges:

  • Zero historical data or proven audiences
  • Need to generate purchases quickly without overspending
  • Identifying high-intent automotive buyers at scale
  • Maintaining stable delivery across multiple campaigns
  • Controlling frequency while increasing volume

Strategy & Execution

To scale efficiently, I built a full-funnel Facebook Ads system designed for long-term growth.

  • Launched structured prospecting campaigns to generate first-time buyers
  • Built retargeting layers to capture high-intent traffic
  • Applied disciplined budget allocation to protect performance during scaling
  • Optimized creative and messaging based on funnel stage
  • Closely monitored frequency, delivery, and cost metrics daily
  • Scaled winning campaigns while maintaining cost control

Performance Outcome

From launch to scale, the campaigns delivered:

  • 964 confirmed website purchases
  • Over 626K reach, expanding brand visibility rapidly
  • Stable cost per purchase between $22 and $33
  • Consistent sales flow across multiple campaigns
  • The account was scaled gradually while preserving efficiency and avoiding performance volatility.

Conclusion

This case study demonstrates how a data-driven Facebook Ads funnel can successfully scale a brand-new automotive parts business from zero to consistent sales. Through strategic structuring, controlled budgeting, and continuous optimization, the client achieved predictable growth, efficient customer acquisition, and a solid foundation for ongoing scale.
